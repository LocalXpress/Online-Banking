Welcome to RCC bank

 About Us

RCC Bank was established in the year 1999. It started its operations from Beleghata, West Bengal. The main objective of the Bank was to provide much needed financial assistance to the mill workers at a reasonable interest rate. Bank earned recognition in a very short span of time and opened its branches in working class residential localities of Beleghata.
